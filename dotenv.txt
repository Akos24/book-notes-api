DB_HOST=localhost
DB_PORT=5432
DB_NAME=your_database_name
DB_USER=your_user_name
DB_PASSWORD=your_password

your_database_name: The name you gave your database, when creating it
your_user_name: The username you specified, when installing postgres.
                By default the username is 'postgres'
your_password: The password you specified when installing postgres. It is unique for you.
